## Chapter 9: The Network

### Scene 1: The Recruitment

- **Key Event:** Angelica forms a resistance network, recruiting Doc Stitch for jamming technology and Ria for ventilation mapping.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Transitions into a leadership role, organizing diverse talents for a coordinated effort.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I need you to build jammers. We need to blind Vane's sensors."

### Scene 2: The First Strike

- **Key Event:** Angelica leads a team to sabotage the Water Filtration Plant in Sector 4, reversing the flow to send sludge to the Zenith.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Have a drink on us, Vane."

### Scene 3: The Ripple Effect

- **Key Event:** The sabotage causes immediate chaos in the Zenith, and Angelica receives an encrypted video message from Lady Vesper.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lady Vesper:** "You have teeth. But be careful. If you bite the hand that feeds you, make sure you sever the artery."
