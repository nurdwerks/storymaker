## Chapter 19: The Interrogation

### Scene 1: The Wake / safehouse, Sector 7

- **Key Event:** Angelica interrogates Joyce using her new "Captain" resonance; despite the high tension, the group eats cucumber sandwiches while Joyce remains bound in Null-Iron silk.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her physical transformation continues as her voice begins to vibrate with a resonance that echoes through the ship's structure.

---

### Scene 2: The Leak / Parlor

- **Key Event:** Joyce reveals that the Founders are ancient mutineers who stay immortal using body-swapping tech and that Lady Vesper (the Merchant Lord) wants Angelica dead to prevent Vane from gaining total control.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She learns that her adversaries are not just politicians but three-hundred-year-old parasites haunting a dying ship.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "They are ghosts haunting a dying machine."

---

### Scene 3: The Plan / Parlor

- **Key Event:** Joyce explains the horrifying contingency plans of the Houses, including "breeding" a new line of Founders if Angelica remains uncooperative.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — The discovery of the Founders' dehumanizing intent for her and her mother solidifies her transition from a victim to a revolutionary.

---

### Scene 4: The Threshold / Parlor

- **Key Event:** Hatcher suggests Angelica reclaim the ship by sitting on the Interface Throne herself; Angelica embraces this path, vowing to "bury" both Vane and Vesper.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She officially decides to seize power, intending to move from a hunted key to the master of the Zenith.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "I am not a player. And I am definitely not the ball. I am the landslide. And I’m about to bury them both."
