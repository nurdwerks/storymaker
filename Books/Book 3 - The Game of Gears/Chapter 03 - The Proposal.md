## Chapter 3: The Proposal

### Scene 1: The Garden

- **Key Event:** Angelica walks through the Imperial Gardens, noting their artificial and highly controlled nature.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Expresses a preference for the "ugly, smelly, but alive" aspects of the Undercity over the Zenith's simulations.

### Scene 2: The Suitor

- **Key Event:** Duke Valerius Grey approaches Angelica and comments on the "perfection" of the Zenith's controlled nature.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Valerius Grey:** "Nature is too chaotic. We improved it. We edited out the rot."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Valerius Grey:** "You are the Sangreal. The Founder's blood. You are valuable, Angelica. More valuable than this entire garden."

### Scene 3: The Spores

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey releases Pax Spores from his ring to chemically induce a state of calm and compliance in Angelica.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Resists the effects of the neuro-toxin by biting her tongue and using the pain to stay grounded.

### Scene 4: The Ring

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey proposes marriage to Angelica, offering a ring that she identifies as containing micro-circuitry for tracking and control.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Valerius Grey:** "I am offering you a leash, yes. But it is a very expensive leash. Most people in this city would kill to wear it."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Rejects the proposal, stating she is "allergic to platinum... and slavery."
