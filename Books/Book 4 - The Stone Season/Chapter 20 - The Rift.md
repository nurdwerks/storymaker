# Chapter 20: The Rift

---

### Scene 1: The Split

- **Key Event:** A rival leader named **Torque** emerges among the survivors, advocating to leave Angelica's group and seek out a legendary "Hydroponics Bay" in Sector 5.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Torque** — Challenges Angelica's leadership with a sledgehammer, calling her a "baby-killer's pet princess."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Torque:** "Sector 5. I heard there's a hydroponics bay there. Food. Water. No monsters. We're not fodder for your experiments."

---

### Scene 2: The Goodbye

- **Key Event:** Half of the survivors pack up and leave Angelica's group to follow Torque, even after Ria warns that Sector 5 is a vacuum-sealed dead zone.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Refuses to use force to keep them, acknowledging their right to choose their own "exit."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Sector 5 is a dead zone. The air scrubbers are offline. You'll suffocate before you get there."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Torque:** "Better dead than lab rats. Better free than led by Zenith trash."

---

### Scene 3: The Silence

- **Key Event:** Angelica watches as the torches of the departing group fade into the distance, knowing they are walking into their deaths.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "They're going to die. I can almost hear them gasping."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "Probably. But they'll die free. That counts for something."

---

### Scene 4: The Burden

- **Key Event:** Hatcher consoles Angelica, telling her that leadership means making "hard calls" and accepting "designated casualties."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Struggles with the realization that she is becoming like Vane—valuing the mission over the individual passengers.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "It feels like losing. It feels like being Vane."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "Leadership satisfies the ego. But it kills the sleep. Get used to the insomnia, Princess."
