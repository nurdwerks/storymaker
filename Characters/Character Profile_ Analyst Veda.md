# Character Profile: Analyst Veda

**Archetype:** The Cold Mathematician / The Predictor
**True Sin:** PRIDE (She believes her algorithms can predict all human behavior and that she is superior to the "unpredictable" emotions of others).
**Virtue Mask:** PRECISION (She frames her refusal to help as "Adhering to the Probability Model").

## I. Role in the Story

Analyst Veda is a woman who uses high-speed computers to predict the outcome of Aris's experiments. She views the world as a series of probabilities.

- **The Prediction:** She tells Angelica that her chance of success is "statistically zero," delivering this crushing news with a flat, bored tone.
- **The Wall:** Her absolute belief in the numbers makes Angelica's struggle feel futile, as if she is fighting against the laws of physics themselves.

- **Impact on Entourage:** The "Prediction" is lost. The research team no longer knows what the goal of their experiments is, leading to a state of existential dread as they realize they are working for a man whose plan is mathematically doomed.
