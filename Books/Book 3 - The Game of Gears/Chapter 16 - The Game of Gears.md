## Chapter 16: The Game of Gears

### Scene 1: The Quiet Purge

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey consolidates his power, merging houses and removing associates, while his new AI Chamberlain reports on the deaths of former colleagues.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Duke Valerius Grey** — Dismisses his past and personal connections as "unnecessary weight" in favor of his future plans.

### Scene 2: The Walk of Shame

- **Key Event:** Angelica is publicly paraded through the Zenith corridors by guards as a "Silt-scum" traitor before her trial.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Holds her head high, noting the fear in the eyes of the nobles who jeer at her.

### Scene 3: The Trial

- **Key Event:** Angelica is tried for High Treason in the Council Chamber; she publicly accuses Vane of lying about the ship's structural failures and his escape plan.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "The hull is fracturing! Vane knows it! He's not purging Sector 12 to save you; he's doing it to delay the inevitable!"

### Scene 4: The Resonance

- **Key Event:** A massive structural tremor shakes the ship during the trial, validating Angelica's claims about the Engine being out of sync.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "The ship is speaking! Listen to it!"

### Scene 5: The Rescue

- **Key Event:** A mining bore driven by Miller crashes into the Council Chamber, and the resistance team launches a rescue mission.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Miller:** "Did someone call for a plumber?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Court is adjourned."
