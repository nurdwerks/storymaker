## Chapter 3: The Drop

### Scene 1: The Chase / Imperial Library

- **Key Event:** Angelica uses Aeon's "Flash mode" to blind the pursuit team and escapes through the library under plasma fire.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She demonstrates tactical thinking, calculating a three-second escape window while under direct fire.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Warden:** "Target heading for the east wing! Cut her off!"

---

### Scene 2: The Window / End of Hall

- **Key Event:** Trapped at a 500th-floor panoramic window with the elevator locked down, Angelica identifies a 2% probability of survival by jumping as her best option.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Faced with impossible odds, she chooses a high-risk leap over surrender, demonstrating her extreme resolve to stay free.

---

### Scene 3: The Jump / Panoramic Window

- **Key Event:** Angelica commands Aeon to use "Entropy" to disintegrate the reinforced glass and leaps into the open air of the Zenith.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She shows total commitment to her escape, jumping into the void without hesitation once the path is clear.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Aeon! Entropy! Now!"

---

### Scene 4: The Fall / Through the Sectors

- **Key Event:** Using a Mag-Lev Grappling Hook scavenged from Solon's office, Angelica intercepts a sanitation drone to slow her fall before crashing through a billboard and landing in a dumpster.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her identity as a resilient survivor is reinforced by her ability to laugh in the face of near-death.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Gravity is just a law, and I am a criminal."
