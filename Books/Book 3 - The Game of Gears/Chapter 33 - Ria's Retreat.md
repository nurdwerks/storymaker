# Chapter 33: Ria's Retreat

---

### Scene 1: The Escape / Palace Ruins

- **Key Event:** The palace collapses around them. Ria provides covering fire while the group flees through crumbling corridors.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — Her combat expertise saves lives as she creates a path through the chaos.

---

### Scene 2: The Sacrifice / Corridor Junction

- **Key Event:** Zephyr spots an ambush. He throws his bag of diamonds at the attackers as a distraction, allowing Angelica to escape.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — His greed is finally conquered by loyalty. He watches a fortune scatter across the floor and doesn't look back.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Ah, damn it. That was retirement."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I'll pay you back."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Yeah. You will."

---

### Scene 3: The Rally / Outer Courtyard

- **Key Event:** Silas's smuggler fleet provides extraction, pulling survivors onto hover-barges as the palace burns.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Silas:** "You Zenith types really know how to throw a party."

---

### Scene 4: The Cost / Aboard the Barge

- **Key Event:** Angelica takes stock of the casualties. Many died in the Obsidian Banquet. Leo is gone. She sits in silence.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Her grief is cold, contained—saved for later when she can afford to break down.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "How many did we lose?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Everyone who mattered."

---

### Scene 5: The End of the War

- **Key Event:** Angelica announces to the survivors that the war with the Founders is over. The new war—for survival—begins now.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "The Zenith is dead. The ship is dying. We either fix it or we die with it."
