## Chapter 49: The Terraform

### Scene 1: The Waking

- **Key Event:** Angelica wakes up in the Chair. She screams as the Source leaves her body.
- **Key Event:** A wave of White Light erupts from the ship.

### Scene 2: The Transformation

- **Key Event:** The light hits the Entropy Fleet. It doesn't destroy them; it effectively "feeds" them. They vanish, satisfied.
- **Key Event:** The light hits the dead rock of the planet below.
- **Key Event:** Clouds form. Oceans condense. Greenery sprouts in time-lapse speed (thanks to Aeon's essence).

### Scene 3: The Landing

- **Key Event:** The **Aeterna**, now powerless and drifting, is caught by the planet's new gravity.
- **Key Event:** It descends. It is a controlled crash. The ship lands in a newly formed ocean.
- **Key Event:** The lights go out forever.
