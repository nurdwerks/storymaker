## Chapter 13: The Silent Bell

### Scene 1: The Lights / Gallery, Museum of Pre-History

- **Key Event:** The museum’s security override flares to 100% brightness; the Custodian droid violently incapacitates Zephyr, hurling him into a display case, while Lisbet is locked out of the system.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She experiences a moment of paralysis as her escape plan disintegrates under the force of Prelate Vane's direct intervention.

---

### Scene 2: The Reveal / Gallery

- **Key Event:** Prelate Vane emerges and admits he killed Angelica’s mother, Elena Valerius, to prevent her from exposing the truth about the Zenith's Founding.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Prelate Vane** — He reveals his cold-blooded history and his belief that justice is a naive concept that led to Elena’s "undoing."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Prelate Vane:** "Knew her? My dear girl, I killed her. I watched the life fade from those eyes you inherited."

---

### Scene 3: The Sacrifice / Gallery

- **Key Event:** Vane demonstrates the power of his "Legion" nano-swarm by slicing off his own ear and allowing the machines to consume it, showing his total control over the swarm via his neural patterns.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Prelate Vane** — His self-mutilation reveals a terrifying commitment to his synthetic creations and a complete detachment from his own physical humanity.

---

### Scene 4: The Goal / Gallery

- **Key Event:** Pinned by the nano-swarm, Angelica is forced to provide a blood sample to the Cylinder, which Vane reveals as the "key" to the Interface Throne.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She faces the horrifying realization that Vane views her not as a person, but as a biological utility.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Prelate Vane:** "You are not a person. You are a password made of meat."
