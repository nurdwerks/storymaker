# Chapter 7: Corinne's Fall

---

### Scene 1: The Refinement

- **Key Event:** Lady Corinne is found hiding in a luxury escape pod that failed to launch properly, still wearing her Zenith silks and jewelry.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Attempts to pull rank on Ria, demanding to be escorted to the "proper authorities."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lady Corinne:** "You there, the armored one. I am Corinne of House Silver. You will take me to my father. Immediately."

---

### Scene 2: The Reality

- **Key Event:** Ria ignores Corinne’s orders and begins stripping the escape pod for parts (batteries, oxygen canisters).
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — Treats Corinne's status with absolute indifference, focusing only on the pod's technical specs.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "House Silver doesn't exist anymore. But these lithium cells do. Step aside, Duchess."

---

### Scene 3: The Hunger

- **Key Event:** Corinne is forced to eat raw synth-paste for the first time when the group refuses to share their limited "real" food.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Experiences a breakdown as she realizes her social capital is worthless in the Undercity.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lady Corinne:** "It smells like... oil. I can't eat this. It's animal feed."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Animal feed is for animals with futures. For you, it's dinner. Eat or don't. The paste doesn't care."

---

### Scene 4: The Join

- **Key Event:** Faced with abandonment, Corinne agrees to follow the group, carrying part of their equipment to earn her share of water.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Shows a cold kind of mercy, giving Corinne a heavy bag to carry instead of leaving her to die.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "You want a future? Carry the batteries. That's your ticket. No work, no walk."
