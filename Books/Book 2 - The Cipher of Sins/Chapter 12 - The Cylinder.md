## Chapter 12: The Cylinder

### Scene 1: The Bust / Alcove, Inner Sanctum

- **Key Event:** Angelica correctly identifies that the Cylinder on display is a decoy and retrieves the genuine Blue Cylinder from a secret compartment inside the marble bust of the First Founder.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her superior intuition and analytical skills allow her to bypass the museum's layers of misdirection during a high-stress alarm.

---

### Scene 2: The Puzzle / Alcove

- **Key Event:** Angelica unlocks the Cylinder by inputting an inverted Fibonacci sequence and passing a genetic scan, which identifies her as a descendant of Captain Elena Valerius.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her bloodline connection to the original ship’s command is confirmed, making her the only one capable of accessing the truth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Cylinder:** "Bloodline confirmed. Descendant of Captain Elena Valerius. Access granted."

---

### Scene 3: The Unlock / Alcove

- **Key Event:** The Cylinder plays a three-hundred-year-old message from Captain Elena Valerius (Angelica’s mother), revealing that the Zenith is a generation starship and the "Founders" were actually mutineers.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Upon hearing her mother’s voice and receiving a direct command to claim the Interface Throne, Angelica’s mission changes from mere survival to a quest to reclaim her heritage and restore the truth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Elena Valerius:** "The Zenith is not a city. It is a ship. A generation vessel... The 'Founders' were not saviors. They were mutineers."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Elena Valerius:** "Find the Interface Throne. Claim what is yours."
