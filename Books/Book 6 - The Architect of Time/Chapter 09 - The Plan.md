## Chapter 9: The Plan

### Scene 1: The Realization

- **Key Event:** Angelica spends a week surveying the _Vanguard_ wreck and identifies its intact keel and nearby mineral resources as the keys to building a new ship.

---

### Scene 2: The Scale

- **Key Event:** Angelica explains to Torin that an alien invasion is coming in twenty years and that they must build a starship to escape.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Torin** — He shows deep fear but remains attentive to Angelica's vision.

---

### Scene 3: The Name

- **Key Event:** Angelica sketches a design for a new ship, the _Aeterna_, which will be built from the _Vanguard_'s bones.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "The Aeterna. The Eternal. Because this time, we are going to last. We aren't just surviving the winter, Torin. We are surviving forever."

---

### Scene 4: The Promise

- **Key Event:** Angelica addresses Ragnar and the elders, promising them salvation through the construction of the _Aeterna_.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She displays the authority of a Commander, delivering a powerful rally for a 20-year project.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "We have the steel. You have the strength. I have the blueprints. We are going to build a way out. It will take twenty years. It will take every ounce of sweat and blood you have. It will be harder than any hunt, colder than any winter. But if you follow me, I promise you: we will never die. We will become the stars."
