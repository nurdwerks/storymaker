# Chapter Summary: Chapter 13 - Nightfall

> [!IMPORTANT] > **Tone Rule:** All summaries must be written in a **Neutral and Factual** tone. Avoid subjective commentary or interpretation—focus on what happens, who is involved, and what is said.

---

## Chapter 13: Nightfall

### Scene 1: The Audit

- **Key Event:** High Chamberlain's auditor, Mister Sterling, arrives at the Night estate with Zenith Wardens to seize it due to unpaid loans.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Mister Sterling:** "By order of the Exchequer... this estate is now the property of the State."

### Scene 2: The Unmasking

- **Key Event:** Droids forcibly remove Lady Selene's illegal "Youth-Grafts," and The Governess reveals she betrayed the family to Vane.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **The Governess:** "Because Vane pays better. And I was tired of raising failures."
- **Key Event:** A Zenith Warden thanks The Governess for her service, then shoots her in the back. Useful tools are discarded when the job is done.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — He watches the woman who raised him die as a traitor, unable to mourn her, unable to hate her.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zenith Warden:** "The High Chamberlain thanks you for your service. Your debt is cleared."

### Scene 3: The Broken Canvas

- **Key Event:** Kaelen witnesses a guard crushing the hands of The Seamstress, the woman who wove the family's garments.

### Scene 4: The Void

- **Key Event:** Sister Void's "mystic powers" are revealed to be stage effects, and she is banished from the chapel.

### Scene 5: The Queen in Rags

- **Key Event:** Kaelen finds Matriarch Lilith Night in the laundry room; she urges him to leave and become a "knife" to cut the throats of their enemies.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Matriarch Lilith Night** — Rejects Kaelen's attempt to save her as a "servant," choosing for him to be an "exile" and a "threat."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Matriarch Lilith Night:** "Go and become a knife, Kaelen. And come back when you are sharp enough to cut his throat."
