## Chapter 22: The Switch

### Scene 1: The Hesitation / "Petal & Stem" - Parlor

- **Key Event:** Kaelen observes the horrific reality of Duke Grey's intent for Angelica—to turn her into a biological factory for the Gene-Key. He reflects on his sister Meridia's fate and his role as a "perfect soldier."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — He realizes that Angelica is the only person who hasn't seen him as a weapon, triggering a major shift in his loyalty.

---

### Scene 2: The Warning / Parlor

- **Key Event:** Kaelen warns Angelica that Grey wants to keep her in a fugue state as a biological incubator. Angelica, horrified, recommits to her suicide pact to prevent this.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her terror at being reduced to a "vessel" or a "function" drives her to a final, desperate resolve to die rather than be taken.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "If they can't control the Captain, they create a Captain they can control."

---

### Scene 3: The Choice / Parlor

- **Key Event:** Kaelen sheathes his sword and dismisses his shadows. He gives Angelica an escape route through the basement's sewage output to Sector 4 and offers to buy her time by holding the entrance against Grey's forces.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — He makes the ultimate choice to serve "the heart" (Angelica) over "the chair" (the Founders), effectively committing social and career suicide.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "It's time I served something real. Something with a pulse."

---

### Scene 4: The Betrayal / "Petal & Stem" - Front and Basement

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey demands Angelica's surrender from outside. Kaelen ignores him, urging Angelica to run. Angelica and her team escape through the basement as Kaelen prepares to face Grey's forces alone.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — He smiles genuinely for the first time, experiencing the "bitter, triumphant" freedom of his betrayal.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "Go, Angelica. Run before I remember I am a soldier. Run before the shadows call me back."
