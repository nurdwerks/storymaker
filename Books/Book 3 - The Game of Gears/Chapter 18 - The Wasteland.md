## Chapter 18: The Wasteland

### Scene 1: The Crash

- **Key Event:** The "Mule" takes a hit and crashes into a mountain of garbage in Sector 0.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Crash:** "Aiming for the garbage pile!"

### Scene 2: Survival

- **Key Event:** Angelica wakes in the wreckage to find Crash's hands have been mangled in the crash.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Crash** — Realizes that he has lost the ability to pilot, ending his connection to the sky.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Crash:** "My hands... Z... my hands..."

### Scene 3: The Ecology of Rust

- **Key Event:** The group navigates the scrapyard, encountering a "Scrap-Stalker" mutant rat and seeking shelter in an old colony ship fuselage.

### Scene 4: The Old Ones

- **Key Event:** Inside the shelter, they are confronted by a group of ancient androids who recognize Angelica's DNA.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Alpha:** "The Maker returns."
