# Character Profile: Lab Assistant Igor

**Archetype:** The Eager Servant / The Hunchback
**True Sin:** LUST (Hunger for the master's tools, approval, and the power that comes with them).
**Virtue Mask:** LOYALTY (He plays the part of the devoted, selfless apprentice).

## I. Role in the Story

Lab Assistant Igor is the frantic, eager assistant who fetches samples for Prelate Vane. He represents the fanatical devotion of those who find identity in the service of a "great man."

- **The Fanaticism:** He worships Vane and treats the Prelate's tools as holy relics.
