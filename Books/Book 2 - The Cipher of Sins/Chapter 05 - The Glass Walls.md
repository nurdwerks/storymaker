## Chapter 5: The Glass Walls

### Scene 1: The Sewer / Sector 12 Drainage System

- **Key Event:** Angelica escapes into the sewers. She encounters a group of Street Urchins—Zephyr's former gang—who recognize her face from the wanted holos.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Street Urchins** — These feral children are the true inheritors of the Undercity, surviving on scraps and cunning.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Urchin Leader:** "That's her. The bounty girl. Fifty million credits."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I'll triple it if you help me disappear."

---

### Scene 2: The Deal / Sewer Junction

- **Key Event:** The Street Urchins agree to guide Angelica to a safehouse in exchange for her promise to "remember them when she's queen."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Urchin Leader:** "You look like someone who pays her debts."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I look like someone who _collects_ them. Guide me, and you'll be on the right side of the ledger."

---

### Scene 3: The Path / Deep Tunnels

- **Key Event:** The urchins lead Angelica through maintenance tunnels that haven't been mapped in generations. They know every shortcut, every trap.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Urchin:** "Adults can't fit through here. That's why we own these tunnels."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I was a Silt-rat once. I remember being small."

---

### Scene 4: The Handoff / Safe Zone Entrance

- **Key Event:** The Street Urchins deliver Angelica to the edge of Zephyr's territory. They vanish back into the darkness without a goodbye.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Street Urchins** — Trust no one, help no one without payment, survive.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Urchin Leader (calling back):** "Don't forget us, Queen. We'll be watching."
