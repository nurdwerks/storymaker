## Chapter 38: The First Test

### Scene 1: The Math Problem

- **Key Event:** The Entity tests her logic. It presents her with the "Trolley Problem" on a cosmic scale.
- **Key Event:** It shows her two worlds: One is efficient, silent, and dead. The other is chaotic, loud, and alive.

### Scene 2: The Trap

- **Key Event:** The Entity asks which world is "Better" (More stable).
- **Key Event:** Logically, the dead world is better. It lasts longer.
- **Key Event:** Angelica realizes it's a trap. If she answers with logic, she loses.

### Scene 3: The Answer

- **Key Event:** Angelica chooses the chaotic world.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Stability isn't the point. Experience is the point. A rock lasts forever, but it doesn't know it exists."
