# Character Profile: Sister Void

**Archetype:** The Nihilistic Mystic / The Oracle
**True Sin:** SLOTH (The surrender of all hope and agency to the "inevitable" dark).
**Virtue Mask:** WISDOM (She frames her nihilism as "Spiritual Enlightenment").

## I. Role in the Story

Sister Void is a hooded figure who speaks in riddles and represents the dark, cult-like spiritual center of the Night Matriarchy.

- **The Prophecy:** She tells Kaelen that "The girl is a blemish on the night," actively poisoning his feelings for Angelica with spiritual dread.
- **The Atmosphere:** Her presence makes the Night palace feel like a tomb rather than a home, reinforcing Angelica's sense of being trapped in a dead, cold world.

## II. Book 3 Integration: The Void

- **Location:** Book 3, Chapter 13 (Nightfall).
- **The Scene:** Scene 4: The Void.
- **The Event:** During the seizure, Sister Void tries to curse the invaders. The squad leader unplugs a cable, killing the smoke machine and holographic projectors.
- **The Reveal:** Her "ancient powers" were stage effects. She is revealed as a fraud with a budget.
- **The Fate:** The guards laugh at her. "Real world calling." She is evicted, powerles and humiliated.
- **Status:** **Exposed / Fraud.**
