## Chapter 6: Squad Iron

### Scene 1: The Sorting

- **Key Event:** In the Assembly Hall, the Headmaster (face a mask of cybernetic silver) announces the stratification system: Gold commands, Silver innovates, Bronze builds, Iron protects.

- **Key Event:** Squad Iron is designated as "Infantry"—the foundation that bears weight and is "buried in the dark."

---

### Scene 2: The Branding

- **Key Event:** Angelica walks to the podium amid laughter due to her small size. A drone laser-burns a barcode behind her right ear—marking her status as "Expendable."

  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Touched the tender brand, recognizing her official status.

- **Key Event:** Angelica surveys Squad Iron: Ria, Jax, and a dozen other thugs who look at her like fresh meat.

---

### Scene 3: The Bunk

- **Key Event:** Squad Iron's barracks (Sector G-4) are revealed as a dungeon near geothermal vents—bare concrete, rattling air cycler, and twelve bunks for fourteen cadets.

- **Key Event:** Angelica claims a top corner bunk and hides her holo of Leo under the pillow—her only piece of soft humanity.

  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Feels the weight of losing freedom; she was hungry but free in the Undercity—here she is a barcode, inventory.

---

### Scene 4: The Theft

- **Key Event:** While Angelica is in the latrine, Ria steals her Mag-Lock Boots—the ones she spent three months rebuilding—and forces her massive feet into them.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Nice tread. I accept these as rent."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Jax:** "Better thank her, Runt. Usually the rent is teeth. Or fingers."

- **Key Event:** Angelica calculates 0% kinetic victory probability against Ria (220lbs with 74-inch reach) and says nothing, climbing back to her bunk.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Mute and weak. You won't last a week, twigs."

---

### Scene 5: The Trap

- **Key Event:** At 0200 hours, Angelica rewires the light fixture capacitor (220 volts) to the bunk ladder using scavenged copper filament.

- **Key Event:** At 0600 reveille, Ria grabs the electrified ladder. A blue arc shocks her, throwing her into lockers. Angelica unlaces her boots from Ria's paralyzed feet and reclaims them.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "The rent was too high. I'm moving out."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Okay. You have teeth."

- **Key Event:** Angelica establishes herself as dangerous despite her size.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "You assume I'm prey because I'm quiet. A landmine is quiet until you step on it."
