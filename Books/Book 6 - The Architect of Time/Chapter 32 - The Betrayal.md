# Chapter 32: The Betrayal

---

### Scene 1: The Navigation / Bridge

- **Key Event:** Navigator Starla plots the final approach vector. The enemy fleet blocks their path to the rift.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Navigator Starla** — Her calculations will determine whether they live or die.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Navigator Starla:** "I can get us through, but the timing has to be perfect. Three-second window."

---

### Scene 2: The Burst / Combat

- **Key Event:** An enemy weapon detonates near the bridge. The nuclear burst blinds Navigator Starla, searing her eyes.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Navigator Starla** — Even blinded, she continues calling out coordinates from memory.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Navigator Starla:** "I can't see! But I know the path—thirty degrees starboard, NOW!"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Do it! Trust her!"

---

### Scene 3: The Sabotage / Engine Room

- **Key Event:** An explosion rocks the engine room. Someone has sabotaged the ship. Intern Oakhart is found dead near the detonation point—framed or guilty, no one can tell.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "Oakhart's body is here. The charges were on a timer."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Was he the traitor or the victim?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Does it matter now? Fix the engine."

---

### Scene 4: The Push / Bridge

- **Key Event:** With Starla blind but navigating from memory, the ship pushes through the enemy line toward the time rift.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Navigator Starla:** "Almost there. Hold course. Hold... NOW!"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "All power to engines. We go through together or not at all."
