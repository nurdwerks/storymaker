## Chapter 09: The Planar Scout

### Scene 1: Exterior Hull / Sector 4

- **Key Event:** A small shard separates from the main Sphere. It is a flat, two-dimensional triangle that spins silently toward the ship.
- **Key Event:** It lands on the hull near Maintenance Airlock 4.

### Scene 2: Maintenance Airlock 4

- **Key Event:** A maintenance crew (Unit 4) goes out to investigate.
- **Key Event:** The Shard activates. It emits a pulse of "Planar Collapse" light. The 3D world in a 10-meter radius is flattened into 2D.
- **Key Event:** The crew members are instantly compressed. They become detailed, anatomically correct diagrams painted on the hull floor. They are dead instantly.

### Scene 3: The Monitor Room

- **Key Event:** Ria watches the feed. One moment, her men are standing; the next, they are paintings.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — She vomits. The violence is so clean, so mathematical, it is more horrifying than gore.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "They didn't just kill them. They... simplified them."
