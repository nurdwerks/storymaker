# Chapter 40: Angelica's Vow

---

### Scene 1: The Reactor

- **Key Event:** The group arrives at the main "Sun Core" reactor; Angelica uses her Zenith authorization to initiate the "Great Restoration" protocol.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "System Override. Authorization: Vance, A. Zero-Tier. Initiate... Life."

---

### Scene 2: The Silence

- **Key Event:** The ship’s hum changes from a low vibration to a steady, powerful thrum; the lights in the Undercity begin to flicker back on, level by level.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Watches the progress of the light on the ship’s map, realizing she has officially ended the "Stone Season."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "We’re awake. The whole ship... it’s breathing again."

---

### Scene 3: The Regroup

- **Key Event:** The survivors gather on the Observation Deck as the ship exits the nebula entirely, revealing a star-filled sky that is no longer hidden by haze.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Steps forward and hands Angelica a handful of "Soil-Seeds" she had hidden in her pockets, representing the start of a new life.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "We need a garden. Not a palace. A garden."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "I’ll handle the irrigation. Turns out a hook is great for digging."

---

### Scene 4: The Final Vow

- **Key Event:** Angelica stands at the glass dome, looking out at the distant, blue dot of Earth.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Makes a vow to navigate the ship not back to the past, but towards a new, unknown destination where they are no longer refugees, but explorers.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "We aren't going back. We’re going forward. We are the masters of the Stone Season now. And we’re never going to let the sun go out again."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "Where to, Captain?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Wherever the light takes us."
