## Chapter 14: The Hidden Door

### Scene 1: The Capture / Gallery

- **Key Event:** Angelica’s blood activates the Cylinder, causing it to emit a powerful golden light that forces Vane's nano-swarm to recoil.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her blood acts as a catalyst for a power far older than the Zenith’s current technology, marking a turning point in the conflict.

---

### Scene 2: The Scan / Gallery

- **Key Event:** The Cylinder unfolds to project a massive holographic starship bridge and identifies Angelica as the legitimate descendant of Captain Elena Valerius.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Seeing her mother in a position of original command bridge authority finally links her Undercity identity to her inherited legacy.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Cylinder:** "Welcome back, Captain."

---

### Scene 3: The Voice / Gallery

- **Key Event:** The holographic log of Captain Elena Valerius reveals that the Zenith is actually a three-hundred-year-old generation ship in deep space, and Vane's ancestor led the mutiny that stole the ship.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Elena Valerius** — Her message provides the definitive proof of the "Founders'" crime, revealing that the entire world of the Zenith is an illusion.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Elena Valerius:** "The Zenith is not a city. It is still a ship. We are still in space... The 'ground' doesn't exist. There is nothing beneath us but the void."

---

### Scene 4: The Reaction / Gallery

- **Key Event:** Overwhelmed by the "miracle" of the bloodline, Vane sinks to his knees in worship while simultaneously vowing to "worship" the blood by destroying the "vessel" (Angelica).
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Prelate Vane** — His religious mania is revealed as he reconciles his worship of the "Captain" as a god with his intent to murder the living heir.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Prelate Vane:** "The blood of the Captain flows in the gutter... Sacrilege and miracle."
