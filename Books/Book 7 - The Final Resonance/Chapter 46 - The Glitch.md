## Chapter 46: The Glitch

### Scene 1: The Runtime Error

- **Key Event:** The Entity glitches. The Botswana cottage flickers into static.
- **Key Event:** It repeats the word "WHY" in a thousand languages. It cannot model a system where components delete themselves for the good of the whole.

### Scene 2: The Breakthrough

- **Key Event:** Angelica realizes the Entity is lonely. It is the only thing of its kind. It prunes the universe because it is afraid of the noise.
- **Key Event:** She touches its hand (the star-silhouette).
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "You are trying to save the garden by pouring concrete over it. Let it grow."

### Scene 3: The Pause

- **Key Event:** The glitch stops. The Entity stabilizes. It looks at her with Curiosity instead of Judgment.
