## Chapter 19: Echoes of Earth

### Scene 1: The Archive

- **Key Event:** The ancient androids, "The Forgotten," lead the group to a server node from the original launch that requires a Ward DNA pulse to unlock.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Alpha:** "The makers return."

### Scene 2: The Captain's Log

- **Key Event:** Angelica unlocks the core and plays a video log from Captain A.R. Ward (2145), revealing the Council's plan to hijack the ship.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Captain A.R. Ward:** "Only my descendants can unlock the path home. If you are watching this... don't let them win. Break the loop."

### Scene 3: The Signal

- **Key Event:** The group discovers a repeating Morse code signal from the Sol System, confirming that Earth is transmitting and waiting for them.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Morse code. SOS. Home."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "We aren't alone. We aren't the last."

### Scene 4: The Revelation

- **Key Event:** The group realizes the scarcity and rule of the Zenith were manufactured for power, and Angelica decides to take the Broadcast Spire at the top of the ship.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Commits to the difficult ascent from the bottom of the ship to the Broadcast Spire to reveal the truth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Then we climb."
