# Character Profile: General of the Praetorian Guard

**Archetype:** The Silent Guardian / The Giant
**True Sin:** WRATH (His power is total, but suppressed into a state of absolute, unthinking obedience).
**Virtue Mask:** DISCIPLINE (His silence is his service).

## I. Role in the Story

The General of the Praetorian Guard is a gold-armored giant who stands at the foot of the Imperial Throne. He is the physical embodiment of the state's military might and absolute loyalty.

- **The Obedience:** He only takes orders from the Emperor, rendering everyone else in the room—including the protagonists—irrelevant to him.
- **The Wall:** He is a statue of loyalty that excludes all others. If Angelica speaks, he doesn't even blink. He represents the silent, immovable wall of the establishment that she is fighting against.
