# Chapter 35: The Stars

---

### Scene 1: The Silence

- **Key Event:** The group stands in silence on the Observation Deck, watching the slow rotation of the galaxy.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — Lowers his sword, realizing the insignificance of his "House loyalty" in the scale of the cosmos.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "The Night House was named after this. But we never knew what 'Night' really meant. It’s not a color. It’s an ocean."

---

### Scene 2: The Identification

- **Key Event:** Using the deck's telescopes, Aris finds a small, blue dot in the distance—Earth—and realizes it is thousands of years behind them.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "That's the cradle. Empty. Cold. We are the last echoes of a dead world."

---

### Scene 3: The Hunger (Existential)

- **Key Event:** The initial awe turns to existential dread as the group realizes they have no way to steer the ship or reach any of the planets they see.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — Cracks a joke to hide his terror, but his voice shakes.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "So... no rescue fleet? No welcoming committee with snacks? Just us and the vacuum?"

---

### Scene 4: The Promise

- **Key Event:** Angelica promises that even if they are just "drifting echoes," they will make the ship their own and find a way to stop the "Death of the Sun" (the ship's failing reactor).
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Reframes their situation: they aren't lost, they are the "New Navigators."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "The ship isn't a tomb until we stop breathing. We have the data. We have the deck. We find the reactor, and we turn the sun back on."
