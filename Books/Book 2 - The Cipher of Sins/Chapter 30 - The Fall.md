## Chapter 30: The Fall

### Scene 1: The Siege / Tower Roof

- **Key Event:** Vane’s Praetorian guard attacks the roof; Hatcher is wounded in the shoulder, and the team is pinned down behind the broadcast equipment.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Hatcher** — Even while bleeding out, he continues to provide covering fire, demonstrating his unshakeable dedication to Angelica and the mission.

---

### Scene 2: The Dropship / Tower Roof

- **Key Event:** Prelate Vane arrives in person on his flagship; his presence radiates a corrupting "Gluttony" aura that rots nearby plants and corrodes the tower’s metal.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Prelate Vane** — His ancient, parasitic nature is physically manifested as he surveys the burning city with a chilling, detached arrogance.

---

### Scene 3: The Offer / Tower Roof

- **Key Event:** Vane offers to spare the team if Angelica surrenders the Cylinder; Zephyr silently encourages Angelica to choose the mission over his own survival.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — He exhibits absolute loyalty, refusing to let himself be used as a bargaining chip in Vane’s attempt to suppress the truth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Prelate Vane:** "Choose. The truth... or your friends."

---

### Scene 4: The Surrender / Tower Roof to Streets

- **Key Event:** Angelica feigns surrender, but Ria distracts Vane with a long-range shot; the team then makes a desperate leap off the hundred-story tower into the burning city below.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — In her final thoughts, she recognizes that while the truth was a heavy burden, it has successfully "armed" the people to fight for their own freedom.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Jump!"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "The truth didn't set them free. But maybe... maybe it gave them the weapons to free themselves. And now, we lead the charge."
