## Chapter 16: Safe Haven

### Scene 1: The Interception / Flower District Plaza

- **Key Event:** Kaelen Night and Squire Pip intercept the escaping team; after Pip is forced to kill a scavenger in self-defense, a conflicted Kaelen allows Angelica to leave.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Squire Pip** — He kills for the first time, experiencing the immediate emotional toll and trauma of lethal combat.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — He chooses to prioritize the emotional state of his squire over his duty to the Crown, allowing a high-value fugitive to escape.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "I said GO! The sector is unsecured. We are retreating to regroup. That is an order."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Welcome to the war, kid."

---

### Scene 2: The Safehouse / "Petal & Stem: Fine Botanicals", Sector 7

- **Key Event:** Hatcher brings the injured team to a botanical shop run by Joyce ("The Needle"), an old contact of his who specializes in deep secrets.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Hatcher** — He utilizes long-standing, covert relationships to secure a location that can provide both medical care and anonymity.

---

### Scene 3: The Sanctuary / Flower Shop Interior

- **Key Event:** Angelica discovers that the shop is an armory disguised as a cozy parlor, with weapons like "Null-Iron" knitting needles and toxin-producing flowers.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Joyce** — Introduced as a master of domestic camouflage whose grandmotherly appearance hides a lifetime of expertise in violence and espionage.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "Television always did add ten pounds of menace."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "I just scrubbed the blood and grime out of this rug last Tuesday, and I’m not in the mood to do it again so soon."
  - **Key Event:** Joyce pauses, momentarily forgetting where she keeps the antiseptic. Hatcher notices the "hollow" silence—a symptom of her Bond eating a memory—and steps in.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "Top shelf, left cabinet. Behind the chamomile. right?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce (smiling):** "Right where I left it. You always were observant, Detective."

---

### Scene 4: The Debrief / Flower Shop - Parlor

- **Key Event:** Angelica reveals the truth about the Zenith being a starship to Joyce, who admits she has always suspected the lie and Warns Angelica that she can never return to her old life.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Joyce** — She acknowledges the heavy burden of the truth, seeing Angelica's knowledge as a force that will either destroy or save her.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "Now you can never go back. Now you know. And knowing will destroy you—or save you. I honestly can't tell which."
