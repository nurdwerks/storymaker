## Chapter 28: The Ascent

### Scene 1: The G-Force

- **Key Event:** The Sphere reacts to his presence. It increases the local gravity to crush him.
- **Key Event:** Zephyr is pinned in his seat. His vision greys out. Bones crack.

### Scene 2: The Hallucination

- **Key Event:** Under the pressure, he hallucinates. He sees his old life—the bars, the gold, the heists.
- **Key Event:** He sees Angelica's face. Not the Princess, but the woman in the dumpster he saved.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — He realizes that saving her was the only transaction that ever mattered.

### Scene 3: The Break

- **Key Event:** He injects a lethal dose of adrenaline. He forces his hand to move the throttle forward.
- **Key Event:** The Mech punches through the gravity well. He is inside the Sphere's outer perimeter.
