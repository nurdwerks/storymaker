## Chapter 06: Martial Law

### Scene 1: The Vanguard Armory

- **Key Event:** Ria issues the mobilization order. She authorizes usage of "Level 5" munitions (illegal kinetic heavy rounds).
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — She manually unlocks the weapon racks. She seems calmer now that there is a tangible enemy to shoot.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "If it moves, shoot it. If it doesn't move, shoot it until it does."

### Scene 2: The Civilian Sectors

- **Key Event:** Squad Iron moves through the residential decks, expecting resistance to the lockdown orders. Instead, they find people huddled in fear.
- **Key Event:** Power is cut to the civilian grid to reroute energy to the main cannons. The lights go out; only emergency red strobes remain.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Civilian:** "Why are the lights gone? Vane said we were safe!"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Vane isn't here. I am. Get to the shelters."

### Scene 3: The Command Link

- **Key Event:** Ria tries to link directly with the ship’s tactical grid, but Vane blocks her clearance. She has to hack her own defense grid with Lisbet's help.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "He's firewalled the main guns. I'm bypassing... got it. You have the stick, Ria."
