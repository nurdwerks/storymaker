## Chapter 8: The Velvet Trap

### Scene 1: The Runner / Alleyway, Sector 4

- **Key Event:** Lisbet’s young runner, Pixel, delivers the decryption key for Solon’s chip despite being brutally attacked by a gang that stole her cybernetic eye.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — She is deeply shaken and vengeful after Pixel’s injury, showing a protective, human side beneath her glitchy exterior.

---

### Scene 2: The Cipher / Safehouse, "The Rusty Gear"

- **Key Event:** Angelica and Lisbet use the key to unlock the data-chip, which reveals a 3D star chart and a schematic of the Zenith that points to the Museum of Pre-History.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her ability to recognize order in the seemingly random "aesthetic" encryption highlights her analytical intelligence and unique heritage.

---

### Scene 3: The Artifact / Safehouse

- **Key Event:** The map identifies "Exhibit 404: The Cylinder of Truth" as the location of the information Solon died for, which Hatcher notes is restricted from public viewing.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Hatcher** — He questions the entire foundation of the Zenith's history, suggesting that the "Ascension" might be a manufactured myth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "They hid the truth in the one place no one looks: The past."

---

### Scene 4: The Preparation / Safehouse

- **Key Event:** The group prepares for the heist; Angelica rebuilds her Haptic Glove into "Shock-Knuckles" while Lisbet identifies a Class-7 Synth-Guardian, "The Custodian," as the main obstacle.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her skill as a scavenger and engineer is proven as she upgrades her weapons using only salvaged parts and scraps.
