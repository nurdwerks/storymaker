## Chapter 10: The Hidden Door

### Scene 1: The Sniper's Secret / Rusted Gantry Crane, Above Highway

- **Key Event:** A sniper named Ghost (formerly Unit 44) kills her former squadmate Kael to remain hidden while acting as Ria's "insurance policy" during the hunt for Angelica.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ghost** — She renounces her former life and name, choosing her loyalty to Ria and her new status as a "traitor" over her past as a Praetorian Scout.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ghost:** "My name is Ghost."

---

### Scene 2: The Ride / Mag-Lev Highway, Sector 4

- **Key Event:** Hatcher's dilapidated hover-car is intercepted by Ria on a black Interceptor bike; Ria hijacks the car’s comms and demands Angelica’s surrender.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — Introduced as a relentless, cybernetically enhanced hunter who treats her mission with ruthless, professional coldness.

---

### Scene 3: The Ram / Mag-Lev Highway

- **Key Event:** Ria rams the car, deflects Zephyr's blaster fire with her cybernetic arm, and leaps onto the roof of the car, punching through the sunroof to grab Zephyr.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — She demonstrates overwhelming physical power and a complete lack of hesitation when confronting her former friend.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Hello, traitor."

---

### Scene 4: The Save / Mag-Lev Highway

- **Key Event:** Ria chokes Zephyr and demands Angelica surrender; Angelica chooses to use Aeon to distract Ria and barely saves Zephyr from falling off the roof as they pass through a narrow tunnel.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She makes a split-second decision to prioritize her teammate's life over defeating her pursuer, proving she still values her allies despite her cold leadership.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "This isn't over!"
