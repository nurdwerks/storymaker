### Scene 2: The Lie / Bridge

- **Key Event:** "The Stone Season," he says. "The Long Dark. The Ice Age."
- **Key Event:** He points to the map. "We were just behind that cloud. That's all it was. A shadow."
- **Key Event:** He starts to laugh. It's a bitter, sharp sound.
- **Key Event:** "A thousand years of sacrifice," he says. "A thousand years of 'The Faith'. The Foundation. The suffering. All of it."
- **Key Event:** He slams his fist against the transparency. "It was just an eclipse! We were praying to a nebula!"
- **Key Event:** The absurdity of it hits them all. The wars fought over resources that were frozen. The people killed for "conserving heat." All because of orbital mechanics.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — Laughs bitterly at the irony: their religious and social structures were based on a predictable orbital cycle they didn't understand.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "A thousand years of sacrifice. A thousand years of 'The Faith'. And it was just a shadow. We were praying to a nebula."

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "He wasn't trying to be a god. He was trying to be a bridge. He just didn't care how many people he walked on to cross it."
