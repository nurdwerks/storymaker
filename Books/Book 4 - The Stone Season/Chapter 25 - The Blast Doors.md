# Chapter 25: The Blast Doors

---

### Scene 1: The Goal

- **Key Event:** The group reaches the "Project Noah" blast doors at the bottom of the ship, which Aris identifies as containing the control codes for the ship's ecosystem.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "This is it. The biological vault. If we get in, we control the weather. We win."

---

### Scene 2: The Horde

- **Key Event:** A massive wave of Blighted converges on their position, tracking their heat signatures down the ventilation shafts; Aris struggles to bypass the Zenith-level biocodes.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "The whole damn ant hill is coming. They must have tracked our heat signatures all the way down."

---

### Scene 3: The Jam

- **Key Event:** The blast door hydraulic fails, opening only a two-foot crack; the group squeezes through as the Horde arrives.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "Hydraulic failure. The pistons are seized. That's as far as it goes."
