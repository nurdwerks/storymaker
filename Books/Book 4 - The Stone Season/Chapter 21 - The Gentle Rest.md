# Chapter 21: The Gentle Rest

---

### Scene 1: The Mirage

- **Key Event:** In the deep foundations, the group encounters a warm, well-lit parlor that looks like a high-end Zenith residence: "The Gentle Rest - Senior Living Facility."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Hallucination? Oxygen deprivation usually makes me see naked dancers, not... heavy oak doors."

---

### Scene 2: The Gatekeeper

- **Key Event:** The group is met by a severe nurse and **Joyce**, an elderly woman who is revealed to be the legendary Undercity assassin "The Needle."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Joyce** — Appears in a grandmotherly cardigan, attempting to maintain a "bubble" of normalcy in the apocalypse.
  - _Key Dialog_ **Joyce:** "It's just poor travelers. Don't be a dragon. Put the kettle on."

---

### Scene 3: The Circle

- **Key Event:** The group sits with the "Sewing Circle" of elderly residents, discovering they are all retired or hiding members of the Undercity elite.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "It's a cover. Statistically impossible. This level of normalcy in a disaster zone is a manufactured construct."

---

### Scene 4: The Discovery

- **Key Event:** While looking for a chess piece, a resident named Henderson finds a combat knife and a flash-bang grenade in Joyce’s knitting bag, breaking the illusion of safety.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Joyce** — Her "grandmother" mask slips, revealing the predator underneath when her secret stash is exposed.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Henderson:** "This is not a knitting needle. And you... you are not who you said you were."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce (The Needle):** "The world is dangerous. I just brought the tools to match it."
