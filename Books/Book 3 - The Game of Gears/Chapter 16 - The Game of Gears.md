# Chapter 16: The Game of Gears

---

### Scene 1: The Court / Duke Grey's Ballroom

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey hosts a lavish celebration. Countess Livia hangs on his arm, whispering gossip while eyeing Angelica with amusement.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Countess Livia** — His rumored former lover marks her territory with casual cruelty.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Countess Livia:** "So this is the Little Mouse? How quaint. Does she do tricks?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Grey:** "She's learning. Aren't you, my dear?"

---

### Scene 2: The Broker / Side Chamber

- **Key Event:** Broker Vix approaches Angelica with an offer—information about Vane's plans in exchange for future considerations. She is Baron Gilded's information dealer.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Broker Vix** — Everything is for sale, including her employer's secrets.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Broker Vix:** "Gilded thinks he owns me. He rents me. And I'm always taking new clients."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "What do you want in return?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Broker Vix:** "Survival. When you tear this place down—and you will—I want safe passage."

---

### Scene 3: The Twins / Gilded's Entourage

- **Key Event:** Baron Gilded arrives with his enforcers: the twins Debit and Credit, identical in appearance and brutality.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Debit & Credit** — They flank their master like attack dogs, eyes tracking every potential threat.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Debit:** "The Silt-girl is staring."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Credit:** "Let her stare. She'll learn."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Baron Gilded:** "Boys. Manners. We're among... peers."

---

### Scene 4: The Dance / Ballroom Floor

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey forces Angelica to perform for the court, demonstrating his control. She plays along, already planning her escape.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Grey:** "Smile, my dear. The Game of Gears requires a pretty face."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "And you're such a pretty player."
