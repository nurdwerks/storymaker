# Chapter 32: Kaelen's Stand

---

### Scene 1: The Lab Assault / Vane's Research Wing

- **Key Event:** The team breaches Prelate Vane's hidden research laboratory. Dr. Splicer and Lab Assistant Igor are among the defenders.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Splicer:** "You're too late! The data is already uploaded!"

---

### Scene 2: The Casualties / Inner Lab

- **Key Event:** In the firefight, Dr. Splicer is killed by Ria's precise shot. Lab Assistant Igor attempts to flee but is caught in the crossfire.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — Shows no hesitation eliminating Vane's scientists.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Igor:** "Wait—I'm just an assistant! I didn't—"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Should've picked a better boss."

---

### Scene 3: The Archivist / Data Core

- **Key Event:** The team finds The Archivist—an elderly man merged with the data systems—who offers to help them access Vane's files in exchange for a quick death.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **The Archivist** — His mind has been shattered by forced integration; death is mercy.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **The Archivist:** "I remember everything. Every experiment. Every scream. Please. Let me forget."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Give us what we need. Then you can rest."

---

### Scene 4: The Stand / Laboratory Exit

- **Key Event:** Kaelen holds the exit while the team downloads the data. He faces a squad of Vane's elite guards alone.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — His combat mastery is on full display as he carves through the opposition.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "Go. I'll hold them."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Don't die."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "Not today."
