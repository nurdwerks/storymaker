# Chapter 22: The Shattering

---

### Scene 1: The Protest

- **Key Event:** Torque’s survivors and local scavengers attack the "Gentle Rest" beacon after seeing its lights; a resident, Mrs. Gable, is killed by a "False Flag" agitator's shot.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "Gable, get away from the glass! ... She's gone."

---

### Scene 2: The Sacrifice

- **Key Event:** The orderly **Timmy** is paralyzed while shielding Joyce from a scavenger's laser cutter, and Joyce goes on a lethal rampage using her knitting needles.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Joyce (The Needle)** — Transforms from a retired old lady into a "whirlwind of steel," massacring the mob with clinical precision.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Timmy:** "Did I stop them, Miss Joyce?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "Yes, Timmy. You're a good boy."

---

### Scene 3: The Betrayal

- **Key Event:** Nurse Ratched reveals she was a mole for the Zenith, sent to keep Joyce "retired" and pacified, and attempts to drug her.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Nurse Ratched** — Displays a cold, clinical loyalty to the Spire, viewing the residents as "property of the state."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Nurse Ratched:** "The Zenith made me an offer. All I had to do was keep you pacified. But active assets are dangerous."

---

### Scene 4: The Escape

- **Key Event:** Angelica saves Joyce from Ratched and drags her into the cold tunnels as the "Gentle Rest" burns down.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Joyce** — Is left emotionally empty and broken, realizing her sanctuary was a lie.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Joyce:** "I just wanted to knit. I just wanted to be normal."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "The illusion was ash. You're awake now."
