## Chapter 1: The Gilded Cage

### Scene 1: The New Quarters

- **Key Event:** Angelica Ward is moved from a laboratory to a luxurious suite in the Zenith Spire.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Expresses a preference for the harsh honesty of the Undercity over the artificial comfort of the Spire.

### Scene 2: The Handler

- **Key Event:** Lady Corinne visits Angelica to prepare her for court, emphasizing her role as the "Lost Daughter" and a symbol.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lady Corinne:** "Magister Vane wants you presentable for the court. This is Resonance-Silk. It changes color based on your emotional state."

### Scene 3: The Makeover

- **Key Event:** Angelica undergoes a forced makeover, including wearing a restrictive memory-polymer corset.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Insists on keeping her steel-toed combat boots for practical survival despite the formal attire.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lady Corinne:** "We fight with posture. We fight with influence. We fight with poison dripped into tea and whispers behind fans. This dress is armor."

### Scene 4: The Mirror

- **Key Event:** Angelica sees her new appearance in a mirror and feels a sense of loss of identity.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Rips a decorative ribbon to tie her hair back in a way that feels more authentic to her mechanic roots.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "A cage made of gold is still a cage. And I am going to break the bars."
