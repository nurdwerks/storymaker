## Chapter 2: The Murder

### Scene 1: The Message / Palace Dining Hall, Sector 1

- **Key Event:** While dining under heavy surveillance in the Palace, a tiny courier drone drops a data-chip from Archon Solon into Angelica’s soup, warning that "The History is a lie" and that she is being sought as a "vessel."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She demonstrates her alertness and experience with surveillance by subtly hiding the data-chip under her tongue without alerting the guards or drones.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Message:** "The Selection isn't about finding a soldier. It's about finding a vessel. Meet me at the Archives."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "I have a feeling that tonight is going to be a very bad night."

---

### Scene 2: The Archives / Imperial Library, Sector 1-Alpha

- **Key Event:** Angelica disables the tracker in her arm and sneaks into the Imperial Library at midnight, only to find the "Pre-Ascension History" section completely stripped of its books.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Her resolve is shown when she performs a painful self-surgery to remove her tracker to maintain secrecy.

---

### Scene 3: The Body / Imperial Library

- **Key Event:** Angelica discovers Archon Solon dead at a desk with his throat slit; the Sigil of the Founders—a circle with four interlocking triangles—is burned into his forehead as an execution mark.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Despite the shock of finding a body, she remains observant enough to identify the surgical precision of the wound and the significance of the brand.

---

### Scene 4: The Frame / Imperial Library

- **Key Event:** The library is suddenly illuminated and surrounded by Wardens, who find Angelica with blood on her hands and a glass dagger—the planted murder weapon—in her pocket.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Faced with an impossible situation, she finds a clue on a blood-soaked scroll and chooses to resist the arrest rather than surrender to the frame-up.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Warden Captain:** "You are under arrest for the murder of Archon Solon. For High Treason against the Zenith."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "History is written by the victors, but it is edited by the murderers."
