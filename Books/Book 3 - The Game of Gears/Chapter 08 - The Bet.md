## Chapter 8: The Bet

- **Key Event:** Angelica travels to Level 50, the Neon District, to meet with Silas, King of the Smugglers.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I'm looking for Silas."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Bouncer:** "Don't stare at the eye. He hates that."

### Scene 2: The Black Gear

- **Key Event:** Angelica challenges Silas to a game of "Void Poker" to secure his fleet for her mission.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I'm betting this. The access codes to the Zenith supply elevators. Bypass clearance."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Silas:** "High stakes. Let's play."

### Scene 3: Dead Man's Hand

- **Key Event:** Angelica wins the game against Silas by cheating with an Ace taught by Miller.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Demonstrates her willingness to use dishonest methods to achieve a greater goal for the resistance.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I win."

### Scene 4: The Raid

- **Key Event:** Zenith Enforcers raid the club, but Angelica and Silas fight their way out together.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Vane is purging the sector! If you don't help me, we all die! Including you!"
