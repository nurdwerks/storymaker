# Character Profile: Sergeant "Brick" Wall

**Archetype:** The Heavy / The Bodyguard
**True Sin:** WRATH (His power is quiet and focused, but absolute in its capacity for violence).
**Virtue Mask:** PROTECTION (He claims his pre-battle rituals are about "Readiness").

## I. Role in the Story

Sergeant "Brick" Wall is a massive heavy-weapons specialist who acts as General Ironwood’s unofficial bodyguard. He represents the physical weight and immovable nature of Ironwood's legion.

- **The Ritual:** He and Ironwood share a quiet cigar before battle—a ritual of warrior-intimacy that Angelica is barred from, leaving her feeling profoundly lonely even in the midst of an army.
- **The Nickname:** He refers to Angelica as "The Sparkler," a diminutive nickname that belittles her power and treats her magic as a triviality compared to the "real" weight of his steel.
