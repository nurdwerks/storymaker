## Chapter 5: The Glass Walls

### Scene 1: The Cost / Cramped Alleyway, Sector 4

- **Key Event:** Zephyr explains the "Greed Bond" to Angelica, revealing that his golden cybernetic arm siphons money from his bank accounts to function; if his balance hits zero, he dies.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — His survival is intrinsically tied to his wealth, making every action a calculated expense, yet he risks his limited credits to help Angelica.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Every time I use this arm... it draws from my account. The arm runs on money, literally."

---

### Scene 2: The Payment / Alleyway

- **Key Event:** Angelica offers her family locket—her last possession—as payment for Zephyr's help, but he initially rejects it, claiming he doesn't take "payment in sob stories."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She demonstrates her desperation and willingness to sacrifice her only memento of her family to save Leo.

---

### Scene 3: The Bite / Alleyway

- **Key Event:** Zephyr reveals his personal hatred for Prelate Vane, explaining that his own sister died in the Selection three years ago.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — He confesses that his assistance isn't out of kindness, but because hurting Vane is the only thing that still feels right to him.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "I'm not helping you because I'm good, Princess. I'm helping you because hurting Vane is the only thing that still feels right."

---

### Scene 4: The Contract / Alleyway

- **Key Event:** Zephyr agrees to provide 24 hours of protection and lead Angelica to help, with the warning that he will sell her bounty if he cannot pay his bills afterward.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She accepts the dangerous and temporary deal, showing her pragmatic approach to survival under pressure.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Loyalty is a subscription service, Princess. Your free trial just started."
