# Character Profile: Major Steel

**Archetype:** The Second-in-Command / The Mirror
**True Sin:** PRIDE (She views her military discipline as the only valid form of humanity, looking down on civilians).
**Virtue Mask:** DUTY (She frames her dismissal of others as "Operational Efficiency").

## I. Role in the Story

Major Steel is General Ironwood’s right hand and tactical advisor. She is a woman of few words who communicates with Ironwood through a series of nods and glances, reflecting his own stoicism.

- **The Connection:** She and Ironwood share a synchronized rhythm forged in battle, a bond of shared trauma that Angelica can never breach.
- **The Dismissal:** She salutes Ironwood with snap precision but looks through Angelica, dismissing her strategic input as "civilian noise" and reinforcing the barrier between the military and the people.
