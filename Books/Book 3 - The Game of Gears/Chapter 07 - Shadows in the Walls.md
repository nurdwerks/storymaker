# Chapter 7: Shadows in the Walls

---

### Scene 1: The Network / Silas's Warehouse

- **Key Event:** Lisbet discovers that the Zenith's surveillance network has been compromised—someone is tracking their communications through her pet AI, Router.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Realizes her own creation has been turned against them.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "They're using Router. They're inside his code."

---

### Scene 2: The Choice / Server Room

- **Key Event:** To save the network and protect the resistance, Lisbet must permanently "wipe" Router—erasing her only friend.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — She kills the closest thing she has to family to save strangers.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Router:** "Lisbet? Why are you crying?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "I'm sorry. I'm so sorry. Goodbye, buddy."

---

### Scene 3: The Aftermath / Server Room

- **Key Event:** Router's processes fade. Lisbet sits alone in the dark, mourning in silence. Angelica finds her but says nothing—just sits beside her.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Understands that some losses don't need words.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "He didn't understand. He just... trusted me."

---

### Scene 4: The Hardening / Warehouse

- **Key Event:** Lisbet emerges cold and focused. She rebuilds the network without Router, faster and angrier.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Grief becomes fuel. She won't let Router's death be meaningless.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "No more pets. Just tools. Tools don't die."
