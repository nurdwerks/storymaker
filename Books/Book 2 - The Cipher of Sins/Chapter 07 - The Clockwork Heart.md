## Chapter 7: The Clockwork Heart

### Scene 1: The Call / Back Room, "The Rusty Gear"

- **Key Event:** Hatcher uses an ancient, untraceable vacuum-tube communicator to call a "ghost" named Lisbet for help with the decrypted data.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Hatcher** — He utilizes obsolete technology to bypass modern surveillance, showing his experience in operative tradecraft.

---

### Scene 2: The Hack / Back Room

- **Key Event:** Lisbet, a high-energy teenage "Glitch," arrives and reveals that Angelica’s palace escape video has gone viral, making her the most trending bounty in the Zenith.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — She is introduced as a hyperactive technical genius who experiences data as a physical sensation.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "Got it. ... Number one on the bounty board. Eighty million views in one hour."

---

### Scene 3: The Ego / Back Room

- **Key Event:** Angelica views the public hate and demonizing propaganda against her; instead of fear, she feels a dark sense of pride in Being a threat to the state.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She embraces her status as a "dangerous person," realizing that public hate can be manipulated into a tool for survival.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "They're afraid of me."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Hate is just attention with a negative sign."

---

### Scene 4: The Plan / Back Room

- **Key Event:** Lisbet activates the "Multiplicity Protocol," broadcasting dozens of fake bio-signatures across the Zenith to confuse the Wardens' tracking systems.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Her brilliance and ego are displayed as she effortlessly dismantles the city's coordinated drone hunt with her custom code.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Fame is just a target painted on your back in neon."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Then let's turn off the lights. We've got a heist to plan."
