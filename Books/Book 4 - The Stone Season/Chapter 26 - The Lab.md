# Chapter 26: The Lab

---

### Scene 1: The Descent

- **Key Event:** The group enters the "Foundation," a massive, sterile laboratory complex filled with rows of glowing white clone tanks.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "It’s too quiet. Even the dust looks like it was staged by a professional."

---

### Scene 2: The Identification

- **Key Event:** Dr. Aris identifies the facility as the "Zenith Gene-Bank," where the genetic material of the Spire’s elite was stored for "rebirth."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Dr. Aris** — Touches the console with a reverent fear, realizing that the Zenith were not just ruling the ship; they were planning to outlive it.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "They weren't just storing DNA. They were storing souls. This is a factory for immortality."

---

### Scene 3: The Discovery

- **Key Event:** Angelica finds a tank labeled "Subject Zero—Vance, E." and realizes it contains a biological match for her own DNA.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Stares at the face in the tank, which is an exact replica of her own, and suffers a momentary identity collapse.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "That's my face. That's my mother's name. But it's... me."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "No. It's a template. You're the copy."

---

### Scene 4: The Revelation

- **Key Event:** A recording from **Elena Vance** (Subject Zero) plays automatically, explaining that Project Noah was designed to create a "Perfect Vessel" to survive the Pathogen.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Elena Vance (Recording):** "If you are reading this, the Fall has occurred. You are the failsafe. You are the stone that will weather the season."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I wasn't born. I was... manufactured. Everything I remember is a lie."
