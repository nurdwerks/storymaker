# Chapter 29: Corinne's Temptation

---

### Scene 1: The Promise

- **Key Event:** The Mother offers Lady Corinne a position as the "New Queen" of the Spire, promising to restore her beauty and status if she betrays Angelica.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Hesitates, her past desires for power and comfort warring with her newfound respect for the group.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **The Mother:** "You were born for silks and commands, Corinne. I can give you a kingdom of silicon and blood. All I need is the headset."

---

### Scene 2: The Choice

- **Key Event:** Corinne attempts to steal the headset while Angelica is in a trance-like state during the mind-meld.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "I'm sorry, Angelica. But I can't go back to the mud. I'm a Silver."

---

### Scene 3: The Interception

- **Key Event:** Zephyr catches Corinne with the headset and pins her to the wall, his industrial hook millimeters from her throat.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — Shows an unexpected maturity, choosing to talk Corinne down instead of killing her based on "Rogue Ethics."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "She’s lying to you, Duchess. She doesn’t want a Queen; she wants a puppet. Look at the Blighted. Those are her 'nobles'."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "At least they don't starve!"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "They don't eat because they don't have stomachs. Is that the life you want?"

---

### Scene 4: The Resolve

- **Key Event:** Corinne breaks down and returns the headset, finally choosing the group over her past ambitions.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Admits her vanity and makes a vow to "earn her keep" in the new world.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "I’m not a Queen. I’m just... Corinne. And I’m cold. Let’s finish this."
  - _Key Dialog_ **Angelica Vance:** "Good choice, Silver. Now help me hold the line."
