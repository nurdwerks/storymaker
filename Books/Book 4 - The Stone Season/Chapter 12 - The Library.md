# Chapter 12: The Library

---

### Scene 1: The Sanctuary

- **Key Event:** Seeking shelter from a radiation storm, the group breaks into a sealed compartment labeled "Historical Archives"—a forgotten library.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Is overwhelmed by the physical books, having only ever seen digital data streams in the Spire.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "It smells like... old paper and dust. This is where they kept the thinking. Real thinking."

---

### Scene 2: The Choice

- **Key Event:** The temperature in the sector drops to lethal levels, and the group realizes they have no fuel except the thousands of ancient books.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Faces an ethical crisis over whether to burn history to survive the night.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "A thousand years of philosophy. Three hundred years of poetry. And we're going to turn it into ash for five degrees of heat."

---

### Scene 3: The Burn

- **Key Event:** Ria begins feeding the first volume into a makeshift brazer, prioritizing biological survival over cultural preservation.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Ria** — Views the books as "cellulose fuel," dismissing the sentimental value.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Words don't keep the blood moving. Heat does. If you want to read, do it fast. The sun goes out in ten minutes."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Burn the medical texts last. We might need the diagrams."

---

### Scene 4: The Revelation

- **Key Event:** While feeding the fire, Kaelen finds a handwritten journal from an early ship Navigator that details the "Great Lie" regarding the ship's destination.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "It says here the stars weren't getting closer. It says we were drifting. We were never heading home."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "Ignore it. The Navigator was clearly suffering from oxygen deprivation. Burn the page."
