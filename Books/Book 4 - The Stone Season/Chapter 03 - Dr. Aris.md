# Chapter 3: Dr. Aris

---

### Scene 1: The Lab

- **Key Event:** The group finds an intact Science Division module from the Spire embedded in a collapsed factory and forces entry.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Uses her neural link to jump the complex Zenith biocodes and open the airlock.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "Encryption is... complex. High-level Zenith biocodes. But the battery is leaking. I can jump it."

---

### Scene 2: The Scientist

- **Key Event:** The group encounters Dr. Aris, a disgraced xenobiologist known as "The Sleepless Eye," who is obsessively working amid the wreckage.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Dr. Aris** — Displays a twitchy, sleep-deprived demeanor, claiming he has bypassed the need for sleep to process data in real-time.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "Don't touch the glass. The condensation ruins the view. Sleep is inefficient. I have bypassed the archive function."

---

### Scene 3: The Bond

- **Key Event:** Aris demonstrates the capabilities of his hard-light construct, Nautilus, which allows him to multitask at inhuman speeds.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "Mutual parasitism is the logical conclusion. I have heat. You have guns. I need bodyguards. You need oxygen."

---

### Scene 4: The Bargain

- **Key Event:** Ria and Aris strike a deal for the group to stay in the lab, though Aris warns them of "guests" behind a dented blast door.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Dr. Aris** — Categorizes the sound of desperate clawing against the steel door as an ongoing "experiment."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Dr. Aris:** "Death? That is a permanent outage. If you want to avoid it, don't open that door. Make yourselves comfortable. The experiment is just beginning."
