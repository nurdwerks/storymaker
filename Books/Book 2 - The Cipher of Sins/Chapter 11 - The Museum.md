## Chapter 11: The Museum

### Scene 1: The Breach / Astronomy Floor, Museum of Pre-History

- **Key Event:** The team rappels from the roof into the Astronomy Floor; Angelica navigates a shifting laser grid with extreme precision while Lisbet loops the security sensors.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She utilizes her Undercity-honed agility and tactical focus to dance through the lethal laser grid without triggering alarms.

---

### Scene 2: The Exhibits / Hall of History

- **Key Event:** Angelica realizes the museum is a site of mass propaganda, featuring icons of her father as a "Traitor General" and sanitizing a history of genocide against the ship's original inhabitants.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She exhibits a deep personal conviction and rejection of the state's narrative, reaffirming her belief in her father’s innocence.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "They made you ugly, Dad. But I know the truth."

---

### Scene 3: The Leverage / Inner Sanctum

- **Key Event:** When Zephyr is tempted to steal a priceless diamond collection to pay his debts, Angelica manipulates his greed by promising him a seat on the High Council once their mission is successful.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She demonstrates cunning leadership, successfully subverting Zephyr’s impulsive greed toward the mission's long-term goals.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "A fresh start in a grave isn't worth much."

---

### Scene 4: The Custodian / Inner Sanctum

- **Key Event:** The team reaches the Cylinder of Truth, but is interrupted by the "Custodian," a nine-foot Synth-Guardian; Zephyr engages the droid in a costly battle while Angelica retrieves the artifact.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — He commits to his role as the team's protector, sacrificing his life-sustaining credits to hold off the superior mechanical opponent.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Every man has a price. Yours is surprisingly cheap."
