# Chapter 24: Angelica's Burden

---

### Scene 1: The Night Watch

- **Key Event:** Angelica takes the night watch on a catwalk over a massive ventilation shaft, reflecting on the growing list of casualties (Pip, Lord Vermillion, Torque).

---

### Scene 2: The Weight

- **Key Event:** Angelica has a hallucination or vivid dream of **Leo**, who tells her that she must become "harder" and stop trying to be a "saint" to survive.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Faces the internal conflict of leadership: whether she is the "Captain" or just "Angie," the silt-rat who fixes toasters.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Leo (Vision):** "Vane was right. You have to be harder. Or you'll break."

---

### Scene 3: The Pride

- **Key Event:** Angelica admits to Zephyr and Ria that she is "turning to stone" and hardening her heart as armor, effectively adopting Vane's "end justifies the means" philosophy.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Accepts her new persona as "Vance, the Commander," rejecting her past identity.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I have to be Vance. The one who sends people to die. The one who cuts her own arm to feed the beast."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Vane would be proud. That's what he wanted. A weapon."

---

### Scene 4: The Resolve

- **Key Event:** Angelica vows to reach the Foundation and the "stars," even if she has to "burn everything she is" to do it.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I will get us to the stars. And if I have to burn everything I am to do it, I will."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance (Internal):** "Heavy is the head that wears the headset. But someone has to wear it. I will carry the weight until it breaks me. And then I'll carry it broken."
