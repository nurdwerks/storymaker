## Chapter 34: The Long Winter

### Scene 1: The Cost

- **Key Event:** The resistance team buries their dead, including Leo and the androids, in the now-open hydroponic gardens.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Establishes her leadership style by working alongside the people as a mechanic rather than ruling as a queen.

### Scene 2: Rebuilding

- **Key Event:** The ship begins to function as an integrated society, with former elites and workers sharing knowledge and resources to repair the hull.

### Scene 3: The Ghost

- **Key Event:** Angelica reflects on her loss of Leo while resting with Miller.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Miller:** "Every time we speak freely, he's here."

### Scene 4: The Beacon

- **Key Event:** Zephyr informs Angelica that the Earth signal has changed to provide specific safe harbor coordinates.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Coordinates received. Safe Harbor confirmed."
