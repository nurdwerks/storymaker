# Chapter 26: The Lab

---

### Scene 1: The Discovery / Abandoned Research Wing

- **Key Event:** Angelica discovers a hidden laboratory containing records of her creation—she is a clone, engineered from Elena Ward's genetic material.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Her entire identity shatters. She is not her mother's daughter—she is her mother's copy.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I'm not a person. I'm a... backup."

---

### Scene 2: The Overload / Lab Corridor

- **Key Event:** Lisbet, still grieving Hatcher's death, reaches her breaking point. Her neural implants surge with emotion, shorting out every light in the camp.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Her pain becomes literal power, uncontrolled and dangerous.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lisbet:** "He's gone. He's gone and I couldn't even—" _(lights explode)_
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "Get down! She's losing it!"

---

### Scene 3: The Recovery / Darkness

- **Key Event:** Kaelen talks Lisbet down from the overload, using his own experience with trauma to reach her. The lights flicker back on.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Kaelen Night** — Shows unexpected gentleness, using vulnerability rather than command.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "The pain doesn't stop. You just get better at carrying it. Breathe."

---

### Scene 4: The Aftermath / Lab

- **Key Event:** Angelica returns to the group, hollow-eyed but resolute. She destroys the lab records. Clone or not, she is Angelica Ward.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Chooses her identity over her origin.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I don't care what I was made from. I care what I do with it."
