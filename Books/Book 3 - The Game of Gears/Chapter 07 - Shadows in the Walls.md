## Chapter 7: Shadows in the Walls

### Scene 1: The Night Shift

- **Key Event:** Angelica escapes her quarters through the ventilation system, dressed in her old mechanic's jumpsuit.

### Scene 2: The Labyrinth

- **Key Event:** While navigating the tight vents, Angelica narrowly escapes a cleaning drone's high-pressure steam jet by finding a service hatch.

### Scene 3: The Secret Room

- **Key Event:** Angelica reaches a crawlspace above the Council Chamber and overhears a conversation between Magister Vane and Dr. Krell about structural failures.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Magister Vane:** "Sometimes, you have to throw the heavy cargo overboard to keep the rest afloat."

### Scene 4: The Horror

- **Key Event:** Vane authorizes "Protocol Omega," a plan to vent Sector 12 to stabilize the hull, which would kill ten thousand people.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Overwhelmed by the horror of the plan, she accidentally alerts Vane to her presence.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Magister Vane:** "We will vent Sector 12. The sudden drop in pressure will stabilize the hull vibrations. It will buy us five years."
