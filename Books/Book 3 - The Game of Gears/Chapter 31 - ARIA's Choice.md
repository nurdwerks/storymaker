## Chapter 31: ARIA's Choice

### Scene 1: The Brain

- **Key Event:** The team reaches the AI Mainframe, "ARIA," which controls the ship's functions.

### Scene 2: The Shackle

- **Key Event:** Lord Cromwell attempts to stop the group by threatening to destroy the AI core, which would terminate life support.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lord Cromwell:** "One step closer and I fry the brain."

### Scene 3: The Logic Loop

- **Key Event:** Angelica uses Captain's codes and logic to convince ARIA to prioritize protecting the crew over "Protocol Vane."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Choose! Order or Life?"

### Scene 4: The Choice

- **Key Event:** Miller shoots Cromwell to prevent him from using the detonator; ARIA deletes "Protocol Vane" and engages "Protocol Life."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lord Cromwell** — Even as he bleeds out, his final act is bureaucratic—he tries to file a formal complaint. The paperwork scatters, irrelevant. He dies as he lived.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lord Cromwell (dying):** "This is... highly irregular... I will file a grievance..."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **ARIA:** "Protocol Vane... Deleted. Protocol Life... Engaged."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **ARIA:** "Magister Vane is located in the Observatory."
