## Chapter 23: The Plan

### Scene 1: The Cylinder Data / Sewers beneath Sector 7

- **Key Event:** Hiding in the "Bile Ducts," the team accesses more data from the Cylinder. A video log from Elena Valerius reveals that the Zenith's "Resonance" is actually a lethal radiation leak from a failing engine core.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She discovers that the entire social and biological order of the ship is built on a literal radioactive catastrophe.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Elena Valerius:** "The 'Resonance'... it's a leak in the primary shielding. ... It's what powers their 'miracles,' but it's killing every living thing on this vessel slowly."

---

### Scene 2: The Gambit / Baron Gilded's Club, "The Gilded Cage"

- **Key Event:** To access the Broadcast Tower, the team needs credits and access codes. Zephyr brings Angelica to Baron Gilded's club, pretending to sell her.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Baron Gilded:** "The Sangreal. Do you know how much the Duke will pay for a breeder like her?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Baron Gilded:** "You taste like copper, girl. Desperation and blood."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Baron Gilded** — Introduced in his full grotesque glory: gold skin, Repulsor Throne, diamond teeth.

---

### Scene 3: The Hack / The Club

- **Key Event:** While Angelica "negotiates" and feeds Gilded's ego, Lisbet hacks his chair's systems. Zephyr accesses the debt ledger to erase his own record.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lisbet** — Demonstrates her skill by breaking Gilded's security while he's distracted.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr (whispered):** "Almost... there... Come on, you golden bastard, keep talking..."

---

### Scene 4: The Fall / The Club

- **Key Event:** Lisbet disables the Repulsor Throne. Baron Gilded crashes to the floor, helpless under his own weight, screaming like a toddler as they escape with the access codes.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Baron Gilded** — Total humiliation as the untouchable king is reduced to a helpless mass on the floor.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Baron Gilded:** "NO! MY CHAIR! GET ME UP! GET ME UP!"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Debt paid, you piece of shit."

---

### Scene 5: The Broadcast / Sewers

- **Key Event:** Elena's log warns that the ship is on a collision course with a gas giant. Angelica decides to use the analog Emergency Broadcast Tower in Sector 4 to bypass Vane's digital control and tell the whole ship the truth.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She shifts her goal from mere survival to saving the ship, intending to destroy the Founders' "PR" with the raw truth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "If we tell the truth, the lie dies. And Vane dies with it. We don't just fight for a sector. We fight for the ship."
