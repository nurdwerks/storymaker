## Chapter 4: The Red Room

### Scene 1: The Snooping

- **Key Event:** During a formal dinner, Angelica excises herself to explore Duke Grey's estate, using skills learned in the Undercity.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Demonstrates her ability to transition from an overwhelmed outsider to a focused and capable thief.

### Scene 2: The Lock

- **Key Event:** Angelica uses a wine-stained handkerchief, swiped from Duke Grey earlier, to bypass a biometric DNA lock on his private study.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Sorry, Duke. But you really should be more careful with your DNA."

### Scene 3: The Archive

- **Key Event:** Inside the study, Angelica discovers "Project: Exodus," revealing that the ship "Aeterna" is a Generation Vessel and that Duke Grey plans to hijack it.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Recognizes the scale of Grey's plan and the risk it poses to the millions on board.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "He doesn't want to save the ship. He wants to steal it."

### Scene 4: The Escape

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey enters the room, but Angelica manages to download the data and hide behind a curtain until a distraction allows her to slip away.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Valerius Grey:** "You look flushed, my dear. I trust the powder room was... illuminating?"
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Resolves to stop Grey's plans, seeing herself as the "key to the ignition" that she must withhold.
