## Chapter 14: Whispers in the Dark

### Scene 1: The Last Supper

- **Key Event:** On the night before the mission, the resistance leaders share a meal of protein mash in the Iron Heart.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Vane wants to be the only history."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Corinne** — Attempts to blend in with the mechanics, forcing a smile despite the poor quality of the food.

### Scene 2: The Armory Heist

- **Key Event:** Angelica, Jax, and Silas infiltrate the Security Sector on Level 40 and use a concussion grenade to stun guards and steal military gear.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Grab everything you can carry. We're going shopping."

### Scene 3: The Cooling Pipe

- **Key Event:** The team swims up the cooling intake pipe against the current, timing their movement past massive spinning impellers.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "I feel like a bacteria in a vein. A very cold, wet bacteria."

### Scene 4: The Ambush

- **Key Event:** Upon entering the Engine Sector, the team is gassed with a neuro-paralytic mist and captured by Lady Vesper and Kaelen.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen:** "I'm sorry. They have my wife. They said they'd cure her rot."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Labels Kaelen a "traitor" before losing consciousness.
