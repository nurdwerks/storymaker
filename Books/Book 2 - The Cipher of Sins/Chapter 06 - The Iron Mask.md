## Chapter 6: The Iron Mask

### Scene 1: The Broadcast / Public Screens, Sector 4

- **Key Event:** On public holovision, Lady Corinne tearfully denounces Angelica as a "traitor who betrayed everything we worked for," calling for her capture.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Publicly plays the role of loyal aristocrat while privately working to save her friend.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lady Corinne (on screen):** "She was my friend. But the Crown comes first. I beg anyone who sees her to report her immediately."

---

### Scene 2: The Bar / "The Rusty Gear", Mid-Sects

- **Key Event:** Zephyr takes Angelica to "The Rusty Gear," a hidden bar for the desperate, where they hide among hackers and smugglers amidst thick hallucinogenic smoke.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She remains vigilant and hides her identity even while physically exhausted and injured.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "Everyone here would sell their mother for a battery pack. You're worth a thousand mothers."

---

### Scene 3: The Musician / "The Rusty Gear"

- **Key Event:** Hatcher, the bar's saxophonist and a former detective, recognizes Angelica, causing the room to fall into an ominous silence as patrons realize her identity.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Hatcher** — He immediately prioritizes security and ushers them into a private back room to avoid a confrontation.

---

### Scene 4: The Message / Back Room

- **Key Event:** Lisbet intercepts an encrypted transmission from Lady Corinne containing Warden patrol routes and safe windows for movement—Corinne is secretly helping.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — Her loyalty to Angelica outweighs her public performance; she risks everything to help from the shadows.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne (encrypted message):** "Patrol gap at 0300. East corridor clear for 7 minutes. Don't die, you idiot."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Corinne. She's still in my corner. Even now."

---

### Scene 5: The Backstory / Back Room

- **Key Event:** Hatcher reveals he was a Zenith Police detective who investigated the disappearance of Angelica's parents and was blacklisted for finding the truth that Vane wanted buried.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Hatcher** — He shows a weary bitterness towards the system that ruined him for seeking justice.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "Vane doesn't leave loose ends. He ties them into nooses."

---

### Scene 6: The Heritage / Back Room

- **Key Event:** Hatcher explains that Angelica's mother was the First Navigator of the original Generation Ship from Earth and that Angelica is the legitimate heir to the Interface Throne.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She learns the truth of her heritage and the reason for the Throne's resonance, forming a resolve to find the Cylinder of Truth and stop Vane.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "I don't solve crimes anymore, kid. I just drink until I forget who committed them."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Hatcher:** "But maybe it's time I remembered."
