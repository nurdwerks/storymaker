## Chapter 2: The Murder

### Scene 1: The Message / Palace Dining Hall, Sector 1

- **Key Event:** While dining under heavy surveillance in the Palace, a tiny courier drone drops a data-chip from Archon Solon into Angelica's soup, warning that "The History is a lie" and that she is being sought as a "vessel."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She demonstrates her alertness by subtly hiding the data-chip under her tongue.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Message:** "The Selection isn't about finding a soldier. It's about finding a vessel. Meet me at the Archives."

---

### Scene 2: The Archives / Imperial Library, Sector 1-Alpha

- **Key Event:** Angelica disables the tracker in her arm and sneaks into the Imperial Library at midnight, only to find the "Pre-Ascension History" section completely stripped.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Her resolve is shown when she performs painful self-surgery to remove her tracker.

---

### Scene 3: The Body / Imperial Library

- **Key Event:** Angelica discovers Magister Solon dead at a desk with his throat slit. The Sigil of the Founders—a circle with four interlocking triangles—is burned into his forehead.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Magister Solon** — His corpse initiates the plot—he died trying to reveal the truth.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward (reading his final note):** "The Cylinder. Find the Cylinder. The truth is in the—" _(blood obscures the rest)_

---

### Scene 4: The Frame / Imperial Library

- **Key Event:** The library is suddenly illuminated and surrounded by Wardens, who find Angelica with blood on her hands and a glass dagger in her pocket.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Faced with the frame-up, she chooses to resist rather than surrender.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Warden Captain:** "You are under arrest for the murder of Magister Solon. For High Treason against the Zenith."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "History is written by the victors. But it's edited by the murderers."
