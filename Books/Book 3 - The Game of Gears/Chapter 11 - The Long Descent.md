## Chapter 11: The Long Descent

### Scene 1: Freefall

- **Key Event:** Angelica and Corinne plummet in a service lift after its brakes and magnetic clamps fail during an escape from the gala.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Brace for impact!"

### Scene 2: The Crash Landing

- **Key Event:** The lift crashes at Level 50, the barrier between the Zenith and the Undercity; Angelica uses a tool to pry the doors open.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Corinne** — Experiences shock and physical pain, realizing her "shoes are just tight" instead of having lost feeling in her legs.

### Scene 3: Neon Nightmares

- **Key Event:** Angelica and Corinne enter the Mid-Levels' Neon District, where they are pursued by thugs after being identified as fugitives by a drone.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Instantly adapts to the environment by tearing her dress and using grease for camouflage.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Blend in. Slouch. Don't look people in the eye."

### Scene 4: The Sewer Gate

- **Key Event:** They escape the thugs by jumping into a sewage outflow grate, descending into the Silt.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "Better vomit than dead."
