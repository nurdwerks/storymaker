## Chapter 07: The Panic

### Scene 1: The Lower Decks / Mess Hall

- **Key Event:** The truth about the "Cease and Desist" message leaks. Panic starts a stampede toward the escape pods (which are offline).
- **Key Event:** **Corinne**, Scrap, and Old Man Rust barricade the mess hall to create a safe zone.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Corinne** — She doesn't scream. She stands on a table, clapping her hands sharply to silence the room, channeling her old socialite command.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "Panic ruins the complexion and solves nothing. Sit down."

### Scene 2: The Medical Bay

- **Key Event:** Lady Corinne receives the influx of trampled and anxious patients. She refuses to let the panic enter her domain.
- **Key Event:** She organizes the nurses with military precision, shedding her usual detached "doll" persona.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Corinne** — She slaps a hysterical man to snap him out of it, then gently bandages his hand.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Corinne:** "Panic is a luxury for the living. You are currently bleeding. Focus on that."

### Scene 3: The Corridors

- **Key Event:** Zephyr is caught in the mob. He uses his cyber-arm to brace a collapsing bulkhead, saving a child. He complains about the strain on his servos the entire time.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Zephyr:** "That's a billable hour, kid. Don't forget it."
