## Chapter 9: The Silent Bell

### Scene 1: The Setup / Back Room, "The Rusty Gear"

- **Key Event:** The team uses a holographic projector to map the "floating island" Museum of Pre-History, identifying its three defense layers: perimeter lasers, gravity-pressure floor sensors, and the "Custodian" bipedal droid.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She takes command with analytical precision, dictating a tactical approach that avoids the floor sensors by rappelling.

---

### Scene 2: The Roles / War Room

- **Key Event:** Angelica assigns mission roles: Zephyr to distract the Custodian, Lisbet to manage digital infiltration, Hatcher as the getaway driver, and herself as the one to retrieve the Cylinder.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She demonstrates leadership by identifying the unique (and often dangerous) strengths of her misfit team and directing them toward a singular goal.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "I'm the Key. I solve the puzzle. I get the Cylinder. And I get us out."

---

### Scene 3: The Conflict / War Room

- **Key Event:** Zephyr tries to convince the team to loot extra treasure, but Angelica shuts him down, stressing that any deviation for greed will lead to their death.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Zephyr** — His desperation for a "fresh start" and financial freedom is revealed as a primary motivator that conflicts with Angelica's mission-first focus.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "A fresh start in a grave isn't worth much."

---

### Scene 4: The Final Briefing / War Room

- **Key Event:** The team finalizes their 0300 infiltration window, with Angelica acknowledging their flaws but demanding they focus on survival over greed.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — She accepts her role as the leader of a broken crew, prioritizing the success of the mission and the survival of the group.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "Focus on the mission. Greed later. Survival now."
