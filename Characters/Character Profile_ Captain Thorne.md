# Character Profile: Captain Thorne

**Archetype:** The Warden of Duty / The Father Figure
**True Sin:** PRIDE (He views Kaelen's potential and noble lineage as his own personal achievement).
**Virtue Mask:** VIGILANCE (He frames his emotional interference as "Perimeter Checks").

## I. Role in the Story

Captain Thorne is the commander of Kaelenâ€™s personal guard and the man who trained him. He represents the weight of the "Night" family legacy that chains Kaelen to his past.

- **The Obstacle:** He treats Kaelen with fatherly pride but views Angelica as a "security risk" to both Kaelen's life and his status as a noble.
- **The Wedge:** He frequently interrupts their conversations with "perimeter checks" or "urgent reports," using his authority to drive a wedge between the two and remind Kaelen of his "true" duty.

## II. Fundamental Change

- **The Event:** Honorable Sacrifice / Death. During a breach of the Night Estate, Captain Thorne stays behind to hold the "Black Gate" alone, allowing Kaelen and Angelica to escape. He dies as he lived: in absolute service to the family.
- **Impact on Kaelen Night:** Kaelen is crushed. He loses the only father figure he ever knew. His grief turns into a rigid, almost robotic adherence to the Captain's training, making him emotionally colder and more distant from Angelica as he tries to honor Thorne's memory.
- **Impact on Entourage:** The "Night Guard" loses its commander. The remaining guardsmen look to Kaelen for leadership, but his grief-driven distance leaves them feeling adrift and vulnerable, shattering the sense of safety Thorne provided.

**Event Location:** Book 3, Chapter 23
**Defining Quote:** _"Perimeter check complete, sir. The Black Gate holds."_
