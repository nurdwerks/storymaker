# Character Profile: Dr. Splicer

**Archetype:** The Mad Scientist / The Eugenist
**True Sin:** GREED (Hunger for total control over the biological code of the human race).
**Virtue Mask:** INNOVATION (They frame their horror as "Survival of the Species in a Dying World").
