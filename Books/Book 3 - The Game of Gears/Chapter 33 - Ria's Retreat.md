## Chapter 33: Ria's Retreat

### Scene 1: The Coward's Way

- **Key Event:** Magister Vane attempts to escape in a silver pod, promising to return with an armada.

### Scene 2: The Intercept

- **Key Event:** Ria pilots the "Mule" to intercept Vane's escape pod.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Ria:** "The Mule has mass."

### Scene 3: The Collision

- **Key Event:** Ria rams the "Mule" into the pod, causing it to lose control and fall back toward the Spire.

### Scene 4: The Crash

- **Key Event:** Vane's pod crashes into the remains of the Council Chamber while Ria reports she is damaged but flying.

### Scene 5: The Wreckage

- **Key Event:** Angelica finds Vane crawling from the pod's remains.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Magister Vane:** "Mercy... I... I surrender."

### Scene 6: The Judgment

- **Key Event:** Angelica decides not to kill Vane, but to banish him to the Silt to survive at the bottom of the hierarchy.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Vance** — Rejects Vane's hierarchy by forcing him to experience it from the lowest level.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I'm going to let you live... Down there. You love the hierarchy? Start at the bottom. Survive. If you can."

### Scene 7: The End of the War

- **Key Event:** Angelica announces Vane's removal to a cheering crowd as Jax notes the war is over.
