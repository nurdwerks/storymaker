## Chapter 13: Mental Shielding

### Scene 1: The Lesson

- **Key Event:** At midnight in Kaelen's oppressive private study, he trains Angelica in mental shielding.

- **Key Event:** Kaelen attacks her mind with a cold needle sensation, sifting through surface memories. He criticizes her loud, projecting thoughts.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "Your thoughts scream. You project everything: fear, insecurity, calculus. A telepath could track you across the sector."

- **Key Event:** Angelica tries building mental walls (brickwork, firewalls of code), but Kaelen smashes through each attempt.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "Too rigid. Bricks break."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "Too logical. Predictable."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "A shield must be fluid. It must be water, not stone."

---

### Scene 2: The Breach

- **Key Event:** Kaelen pushes harder, drilling past fear, pride, and equations to find Angelica's Core Memory.

- **Key Event:** He sees a memory of Leo in the Undercity on a foam mattress, pale and shivering, whispering "Angel, I'm hungry." Kaelen feels her desperate, terrified love.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "A sibling. That is your weakness. That is the leverage point."

---

### Scene 3: The Backlash

- **Key Event:** Angelica's defensive rage snaps. She grabs his mental tendril and reverses the connection through the Soul-Link.

- **Key Event:** Angelica surfs the Blood Pact link back into Kaelen's mind.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "No!"

---

### Scene 4: The Vision

- **Key Event:** Angelica enters Kaelen's nightmare memory: a dark room smelling of blood and gardenias. Matriarch Lilith Night stands over eight-year-old Kaelen.

- **Key Event:** In the memory, Lilith presses a glowing soldering iron to the boy's chest to "cauterize the leak" of emotion.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Matriarch Lilith Night:** "You are too emotional, Kaelen. Emotion is a leak in the hull. It sinks the ship."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Matriarch Lilith Night:** "I will cauterize the leak."

- **Key Event:** Kaelen throws Angelica out of his head with violent telekinesis. He is shaking, clutching his chest where his mother burned him.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "Get out."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "I saw her. Your mother. She... she tortured you."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Kaelen Night:** "GET OUT!"

- **Key Event:** Angelica now holds a weapon—she knows his secret.

  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Vance:** "Stay out of my head, Night. Or I will remove yours."
