## Chapter 18: Imperial Voice

### Scene 1: The Realization / Flower Shop - Parlor

- **Key Event:** As Joyce prepares to kill the group, Angelica feels a "Gene-Key" awakening in her blood—a physical manifestation of her command heritage triggered by the Cylinder.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Faced with immediate death, she transitions from a fugitive to a figure of absolute power as her genetic memories activate.

---

### Scene 2: The Command / Flower Shop - Parlor

- **Key Event:** Angelica uses the "Captain's Voice"—a bio-coded frequency—to override Joyce’s sleeper conditioning and force her into total submission.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She discovers she possesses an unassailable authority over the ship's "crew," which she uses with a cold and lethal focus.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "JOYCE. SIT. RELEASE WEAPON. SUBMIT."

---

### Scene 3: The Effect / Flower Shop - Parlor

- **Key Event:** Joyce is physically paralyzed by the command, and Angelica explains that the Gene-Key acts as an "admin password" inherited from the original Captain.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She begins to understand that she is not just a participant in the war but the rightful owner of the entire Zenith.

---

### Scene 4: The Aftermath / Flower Shop - Parlor

- **Key Event:** Under interrogation, Joyce reveals that Lady Vesper—the Merchant Lord—wants Angelica dead to prevent Prelate Vane from using her to override and control the ship's core.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — She realizes she is the pawn in a conflict between two ancient monsters (Vane and Vesper) and begins to embrace the dangerous possibility of her own power.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica:** "I spoke. And the world obeyed. I think I like this."
