## Chapter 2: Lord Cromwell

### Scene 1: The Summons

- **Key Event:** Angelica is escorted to the Office of the Steward at the Apex of the Spire, which is filled with archaic books and items.

### Scene 2: The Spider

- **Key Event:** Angelica meets Lord Cromwell and observes his mechanical bond, The Scriptor, writing in a ledger.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lord Cromwell:** "Your file is... messy. Arson. Theft of state property. Treason. Unauthorized modification of Class-A technology."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Asserts her ambition and dislike for "broken things."

### Scene 3: The Threat

- **Key Event:** Cromwell uses a holographic display to show Leo Ward in a detention cell as leverage against Angelica.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lord Cromwell:** "I am the Steward. My job is to ensure the numbers balance. And right now, you are a deficit. You are noise in my system."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lord Cromwell:** "You behave, he eats. You rebel, he starves. It is simple calculus."

### Scene 4: The Choice

- **Key Event:** Angelica realizes the "banality of evil" in Cromwell's bureaucratic approach and decides to comply for now.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Recognizes that violence will not work against Cromwell and chooses a more controlled response.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "I hear you. But sound travels both ways, my Lord. And even whispers can start avalanches."
