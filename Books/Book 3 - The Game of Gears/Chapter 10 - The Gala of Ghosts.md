# Chapter 10: The Gala of Ghosts

---

### Scene 1: The Auction / Grand Ballroom, Sector 1

- **Key Event:** Baron Gilded hosts a black-market auction for Zenith artifacts. Duke Grey attends, using his Pax Spores to "encourage" bidders.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Baron Gilded:** "The next item: a genuine Pre-Ascension water filter. Starting bid: fifty thousand credits."

---

### Scene 2: The Obsidian Circle / Private Alcove

- **Key Event:** Lady Vesper gathers conspirators in a hidden alcove—nobles who secretly oppose Duke Grey's growing power. This is the first meeting of the "Obsidian Circle."
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Lady Vesper** — Revealed as the spider at the center of the anti-Grey conspiracy.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Vesper:** "Grey thinks he owns us. He owns debt. We own knives."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Lord Ashford:** "And if we fail?"
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Vesper:** "Then we die quickly instead of slowly. Choose."

---

### Scene 3: The Watch / Ballroom Floor

- **Key Event:** Angelica observes the Obsidian Circle meeting from across the room, filing away the information for later use.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Angelica Ward** — Begins building her mental map of political alliances and vulnerabilities.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward (to herself):** "They hate each other more than they hate Grey. That's useful."

---

### Scene 4: The Dance / Ballroom

- **Key Event:** Duke Grey forces Angelica to dance, using the opportunity to demonstrate his ownership in front of the court.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Duke Grey** — His charm is a performance, his control absolute.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Grey:** "Smile, my dear. The court is watching. They must see you are happy."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Angelica Ward:** "They see what you want them to see."
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Duke Grey:** "Exactly."

---

### Scene 5: The Forbidden Lineage / The Archives

- **Key Event:** Master Archivist Sol discovers a record in the deep archives suggesting Angelica’s lineage is not "Silt" but part of an ancient Zenith bloodline. He hides the record.
  - _Character Defining Moment:_ **Master Archivist Sol** — Chooses to protect the secret (and himself) rather than reveal the truth to Kaelen immediately.
  - _Key Dialog:_ **Sol:** "Knowledge is dangerous. Ignorance is safety."
